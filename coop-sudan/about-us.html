About Us

The Co-operative Bank of South Sudan (COBSS) is a joint venture between the government of the Republic of South Sudan and the Co-operative Bank of Kenya with the aim of bringing about economic empowerment to the people of South Sudan through the establishment and growth of a strong co-operative movement in every sector of the economy.

Strategic partnerships

One of the Partners of this joint venture, the Co-operative Bank of Kenya, (COPK) is Kenya's 3rd largest Bank with over 4.7 Million customers and an asset base of over 2.7 Billion Dollars. COPK has implemented a universal Banking model which has been recognized and awarded by the Financial Times of London, as an innovative and effective ﬁnancial solution to various sectors
