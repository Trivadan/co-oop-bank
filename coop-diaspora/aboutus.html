About Us

Co-op Diaspora Banking is specially designed to fulfill the needs of Kenyans in Diaspora who want safe, flexible and convenient banking back home in Kenya. It allows those in the diaspora the same banking opportunities that they would have access to in Kenya with a special focus on Investments.

We have confidence that this offering will provide an unbeatable value proposition to help you maintain connections with Kenya.
